Frequently Asked Questions

1

How could I accept a job using MsQArtisan?

To accept a job order in the app, click the job order details and you will be redirected to another view showing the full details of the job order. Click the Accept Job button if you want to proceed and Pass button if you don't want to. Then click the Start Service button if you decide to push through the job and always take note of the schedule. You could trace your accepted jobs in the Tracker Module > Accepted.

2

Could I cancel an accepted job order?

Once you clicked the Start Service button, you could not cancel it anymore. If you do not want to start the job order, click the Reject button and the job order will be stored in Tracker > Rejected. That job will still be available for other job seekers.

3

What do I need to do after my job service?

After the service, visit your Tracker module. Go to Accepted and click the FINISH button. Your job service will be noted and tracked as COMPLETED.

4

Could I accept more than 1 job a day?

Yes. You could accept as many jobs as you can but with different schedules. Also take note of the location to ensure that you could start the job order on time.

5

How could I cash out my balance in MsQArtisan?

To cash out your balance in the app, go to FINANCE > CASH OUT and click the GCash image. Enter your GCash PIN and cash out your available balance.

7

I forgot my log-in password in my MsQArtisan account, how could I change it?

To reset your MsQArtisan password, go to the Sign In Page > click Forgot Password. You will be redirected to a view asking for an email address and click submit. Visit the email address you provide. Click the link sent to your email and there you can reset your MsQArtisan Password.
